The Most Popular Ones In 2022

Ahlgrens Bilar

Ahlgrens Bilar is a popular Swedish candy known for its car-shaped gummies. These chewy treats come in various flavors such as raspberry, lime, and tutti-frutti. The gummies have a soft, marshmallow-like texture and are enjoyed by both children and adults. The candy is available in different variations, including sour versions, foam-filled cars, and limited-edition flavors. It is sought after by tourists as a unique souvenir. Overall, Ahlgrens Bilar is a beloved candy that brings joy with its fun shape, delicious flavors, and cultural significance.

Hallon Lakrits Skallar

Hallon Lakrits Skallar is a Swedish candy that features a combination of raspberry and licorice flavors. These skull-shaped candies have a chewy texture and offer a unique taste experience. The candy combines fruity sweetness from raspberry with a hint of savory richness from licorice, creating a delightful blend of flavors. Hallon Lakrits Skallar is a popular choice among candy lovers in Sweden and is commonly enjoyed during special occasions. The candy is available in various stores and provides a distinct and memorable flavor combination for those who indulge in it.

Gott & Blandat Orginal

Gott & Blandat is a Swedish candy brand that offers a diverse assortment of sweets. The candies come in various shapes, colors, and flavors, providing a delightful mix in each package. The collection typically includes chewy candies, gummies, and foams. The candies feature a range of flavors, including fruity, sour, and sweet tastes. Popular options include soft foam candies in different shapes and flavors, as well as tangy sour candies. Gott & Blandat candies are widely available in Sweden, adding sweetness and fun to different occasions.

Fizzypop

Fizzypop is a Swedish candy produced by Malaco. It features small, bite-sized pieces with a unique fizzy powder coating. The candy comes in various shapes and colors and offers a range of fruity flavors such as strawberry, apple, and lemon. When consumed, the fizzy powder creates a tingling and refreshing sensation in the mouth, adding an exciting twist to the taste experience. Fizzypop candies are enjoyed by both children and adults and can be found in candy stores and supermarkets throughout Sweden.
